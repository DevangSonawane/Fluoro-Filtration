**String Wound Cartridges**

- ID: 28mm/30mm; OD: 63mm/115mm
- Lengths: 9.75", 9.87", 10", 20", 30", 40"

- Removal ratings: 1, 2, 5, 10, 20, 50 µm

**Membrane Pleated Cartridges**

**Carbon Cartridges**
